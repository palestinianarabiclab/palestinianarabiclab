Learn Palestinian Arabic Online

Palestinian Arabic is the everyday spoken Arabic used in Palestine and by many Palestinian communities around the world. This site helps beginners practice useful phrases, greetings, family words, food vocabulary, and simple conversations.

What You Can Practice

You can start with greetings, pronunciation, vocabulary, short dialogues, translation practice, and review activities. The lessons focus on spoken communication rather than formal textbook Arabic.

Who This Is For

The lessons are useful for travelers, heritage learners, partners and family members, students of Levantine Arabic, and anyone who wants to understand Palestinian conversations more naturally.
