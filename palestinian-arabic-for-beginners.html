Palestinian Arabic for Beginners

If you are new to Palestinian Arabic, begin with the phrases you can use every day: hello, how are you, thank you, where are you from, and simple questions about family, food, and plans.

Beginner Path

Start with greetings, then learn family vocabulary, food and drink, daily routines, and short conversations. Repeat phrases out loud and practice small sentences instead of memorizing isolated words only.
