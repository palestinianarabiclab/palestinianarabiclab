Free Palestinian Arabic Lessons for Beginners

Palestinian Arabic Lab is a free learning resource for students who want to understand spoken Palestinian Arabic in real situations. The lessons focus on useful phrases, short dialogues, vocabulary, pronunciation, grammar patterns, and practice tasks.

What You Can Study

Start with greetings and introductions, then move into family, food and drink, daily routines, shopping, health, work, feelings, opinions, and future plans. Each lesson is designed to help you recognize common Palestinian expressions and use them in simple conversations.

Who This Site Is For

This site is for Arabic learners, heritage learners, travelers, language teachers, and anyone interested in spoken Palestinian Arabic. You can browse the lessons without creating an account and book a trial lesson when you want guided practice.
