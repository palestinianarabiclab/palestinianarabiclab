Contact Palestinian Arabic Lab

Use this page for questions about the free Palestinian Arabic lessons, guided speaking practice, trial lesson booking, or corrections to the learning material. Clear contact information helps learners understand who is responsible for the site and how to reach the teacher or site owner.

Lesson Questions

If you are studying the free lessons and something is unclear, include the level, unit name, and lesson section in your message. For example, mention whether the question is about greetings, family vocabulary, pronunciation, translation practice, or dialogue choices. This makes it easier to improve the lesson for future learners.

Trial Lessons

Learners who want guided practice can use the booking link to request a trial lesson. A trial lesson is useful if you want help with pronunciation, sentence building, speaking confidence, or choosing a realistic study plan for Palestinian Arabic.

Book a trial lesson

Course Access

If you want to study independently, you can ask for lifetime access to the Palestinian Arabic starter course. Course access is separate from private lessons, so you can choose the self-study course only, private lessons only, or both together.

View course options

Site Feedback

Feedback is welcome when a page is hard to read, a link is broken, an exercise does not load, or an Arabic phrase needs review. Palestinian Arabic varies by city, village, family, and context, so corrections and notes about natural usage are valuable.

Advertising and Privacy

Palestinian Arabic Lab may test advertising only in ways that keep the learning experience readable and respectful. Ads should not be placed on empty screens, profile selection, modals, error states, or navigation-only screens. For data details, read the Privacy Policy.
