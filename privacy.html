Palestinian Arabic Lab

Privacy Policy

Palestinian Arabic Lab provides free educational content for learning spoken Palestinian Arabic. This policy explains what information may be collected when visitors read the learning pages, use the interactive lesson app, book a trial lesson, or send feedback.

Information You Provide

The free learning pages can be used without creating an account. If you create a local student profile in the lesson app, the name and learning settings you enter are used to personalize the lesson experience on your device. If you book a lesson or contact the teacher, the booking form may ask for details such as your name, email address, phone number, preferred lesson time, and notes about your learning goals.

Local Progress

Lesson progress may be stored locally in your browser. This helps the site remember the profile, completed sections, saved lesson spot, and similar study preferences on the same device. Local progress is intended to make repeated practice easier. You can clear this information by clearing your browser storage for this site.

Analytics

The site may use privacy-conscious analytics and performance tools to understand general usage patterns, page performance, and errors. Analytics help identify which pages need improvement, whether pages load correctly, and how learners move between public content pages and the lesson app. Analytics data is used in aggregate and is not intended to identify individual learners personally.

Advertising

Palestinian Arabic Lab may display advertising in the future to support the free learning material. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, prevent fraud, limit repeated ads, and show relevant ads. During site review periods, advertising may be disabled or limited so that ads do not appear on empty screens, modals, profile selection, error pages, or navigation-only screens.

External Links

The site may link to external services, including a booking page for trial lessons. External sites have their own privacy practices. Review their policies before submitting personal information through those services.

Children

The site is designed for general language learning. Younger learners should use booking or contact forms with help from a parent, guardian, or teacher.

Contact

For privacy questions, lesson questions, or site feedback, use the contact page or the booking options linked from the site.
