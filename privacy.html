Palestinian Arabic Lab

Privacy Policy

Palestinian Arabic Lab provides free educational content for learning spoken Palestinian Arabic. This policy explains what information may be collected when visitors use the site.

Information We Collect

The learning pages can be used without creating an account. If you book a lesson or contact the teacher, we may collect the name, email address, phone number, lesson time, and notes you submit.

Advertising and Analytics

The site may display ads from advertising partners such as Google AdSense. Advertising and analytics services may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, prevent fraud, and show relevant ads.

Local Progress

Lesson progress for visitors may be stored locally in the browser. This helps the site remember completed sections on the same device.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options on the booking or trial lesson section of the site.
