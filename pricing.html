Payment and Course Access

Use this page to request full access to the interactive course or book your free online trial lesson. Payment details are handled directly with the teacher through PayPal.

Self-Study Course Access

This option is for learners who want to study independently. The starter course includes interactive beginner units, vocabulary, dialogues, grammar notes, translation exercises, homework prompts, and review activities. Free preview units stay open so you can try the course before asking for full access.

The course access amount can change during offers or course updates, so the teacher will confirm the current amount before payment.

Private Online Lessons

This option is for learners who want speaking practice, pronunciation correction, a personal study plan, and live feedback. The first trial lesson is free so you can experience the teaching style before choosing a paid package.

After the free trial lesson, you can discuss the best lesson plan directly with the teacher.

Course + Lessons Bundle

This is the best option if you want structure and feedback together. You get access to the self-study course, then use private lessons to practice speaking, fix pronunciation, and review homework. This helps you learn between lessons instead of waiting for the next live session.
