# Palestinian Arabic Lab - Curriculum Blueprint

## Sequencing Rule

Every example must use only:

1. The target item currently being introduced.
2. Vocabulary already introduced earlier in the same unit.
3. Vocabulary explicitly taught in earlier units.
4. Proper names or transparent classroom labels when absolutely necessary.

Words that return from an earlier unit are labeled as review, not counted as new vocabulary.

The learning cycle is:

`Introduce -> guided example -> controlled reuse -> cumulative review -> real-life production`

## Unit Progression

| Unit | Communication goal | New grammar focus | Required cumulative review |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Greetings | Meet someone and exchange basic information | subject pronouns, question words, simple nominal sentences | none |
| 2 Family | Describe family and relationships | possession, gender, dual, common plural patterns | greetings, name, origin, residence |
| 3 Daily Routine | Describe a normal day | present tense, time phrases, frequency adverbs | pronouns, family, basic questions |
| 4 Food & Drink | Order and discuss food | adjective agreement, negation, numbers with food | present verbs, family meals, time |
| 5 Transportation | Ask for routes and travel | commands, prepositions, direction phrases | time, routine verbs, prices |
| 6 Work & Study | Explain schedule and responsibilities | present conjugation expansion, active participles | routine, transport, time |
| 7 Weather | Make small talk and compare conditions | comparison, non-human agreement introduction | feelings, time, present verbs |
| 8 Shopping | Buy, count, compare, negotiate | number-counted noun, adjective agreement | food, prices, comparison |
| 9 Health | Describe symptoms and understand advice | body possession, commands, لازم | feelings, time, past reference |
| 10 Apartment | Describe a home and problems | prepositions, possession, passive participle introduction | numbers, comparison, complaints |
| 11 Opinions | Express and support an opinion | comparatives, لأن / بس / مع إنه | adjectives, feelings, negation |
| 12 Complaints | Report what happened and request action | past tense consolidation, passive participles | apartment, service, politeness |
| 13 Plans | Arrange and change plans | future, intention, negative commands | time/date, transport, weather |
| 14 Hobbies | Discuss habits and frequency | adverbs, verbal nouns, present review | routine, plans, preferences |
| 15 Feelings | Explain emotional causes and events | كان, past review, connected narration | all prior grammar and vocabulary |

## Vocabulary Accounting

Each unit will track three groups:

- New: first formal introduction.
- Review: learned previously and intentionally recycled.
- Support: unavoidable low-priority words shown with immediate meaning, never tested as if already learned.

## Exercise Pattern Per Unit

1. Recognition: meaning and matching.
2. Controlled form: conjugation, agreement, fill-in, correction.
3. Translation: short old-plus-new sentences.
4. Cumulative review: at least 30 percent from earlier units.
5. Real use: dialogue, role-play, short recording, or writing.
