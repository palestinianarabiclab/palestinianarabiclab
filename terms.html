Terms of Use

By using Palestinian Arabic Lab, you agree to use the lessons, exercises, booking tools, and contact options responsibly. These terms are written to keep the learning material useful, accurate, and respectful for visitors.

Educational Content

Palestinian Arabic Lab provides language-learning material for personal study. The site includes beginner lessons, vocabulary, pronunciation notes, dialogues, translation practice, review exercises, and public reading guides. The material is provided for learning support and may be updated, expanded, corrected, or reorganized over time.

No Guarantee of Fluency

Language learning depends on practice, repetition, feedback, and exposure. The site can help you study Palestinian Arabic, but it does not guarantee fluency or a specific learning result. For speaking feedback, pronunciation correction, and a personal study plan, learners may choose to book guided practice.

Bookings

Trial lesson availability may change. A booking is confirmed only when the booking service or teacher provides confirmation. If you submit incorrect contact information or miss a scheduled time, the lesson may need to be rescheduled.

Acceptable Use

Do not misuse the site, attempt to interfere with its scripts, submit false booking details, copy large parts of the material for republication, or use the content in a misleading way. Short references for personal study or classroom discussion are welcome when they point learners back to the original site.

Advertising

The site may include third-party advertisements in the future. Do not click advertisements in an artificial, automated, or misleading way. Advertising should support the free learning content without interrupting core lessons or appearing on screens that do not contain meaningful publisher content.

Accuracy and Dialect Variation

Palestinian Arabic varies by region, family, generation, and context. The site aims to teach useful spoken examples, but there may be more than one natural way to say the same idea. Corrections and feedback are welcome through the contact page.

Contact

Questions about these terms, lesson content, or site behavior can be sent through the contact page or booking options linked from the site.
